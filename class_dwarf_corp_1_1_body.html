Detailed Description

This is a special kind of component which has a position and orientation. The global position of an object is computed from its local position relative to its parent. This is known as a "scene graph". Bodies also live inside an octree for faster access to colliding or nearby objects. Basically anything that has a position in the real world should be a body.
