Detailed Description

Every element in the GUI is a GUI component. Components have children and parents. They have rectangles where they exist on the screen. They may be clicked or accept other inputs.

§ SizeMode

enum DwarfCorp.GUIComponent.SizeMode
strong

This defines the behavior of the component whenever it is inside a layout.

Enumerator
Fixed 

Fixed components do not change their size while in layouts.

Fit 

Fitted components maximize their size to fit inside layouts.
