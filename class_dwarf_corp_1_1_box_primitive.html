These are a set of indices in which the top face of the box is flipped. What do I mean by this? Normally, the indices are like this: 1 ---— 2 |. | | . | | . | | . | 3-----—4 The flipped indices are like this: 1 ---— 2 | .| | . | | . | | . | 3-----—4 Why is this necessary?
